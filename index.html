Code Thinker: Level 1

Spot the Bug 🐞

A "bug" is a mistake in instructions. The computer follows them anyway — even if they're wrong!

🍞 Example Bug: Steps to make toast

1 Put bread in toaster
2 Push lever down
3 Wait 2 minutes
4 Take toast out
5 Put jam on toast 🐞 BUG!

Answer: The toast is still hot. You might burn your fingers.

Better step 5: "Let toast cool, then put jam on."

Bug: Steps 2 and 3 are backwards! If you pour milk first, the cereal gets soggy.

Fix: Pour cereal first, then milk.

💡 Remember

Computers never "fix" bugs by themselves.
They do EXACTLY what you tell them — even if it's wrong!
Finding bugs is called debugging.

Code Thinker: Level 1

More Bugs to Squash 🐞

Find the bug. Explain what's wrong. Fix it.

🐞

BUG 1: Steps to tie shoes

1Cross the laces
2Make a loop with one lace
3Make a loop with the other lace
4Pull both loops tight

Bug: You never actually tied a knot! Two loose loops won't stay together.

Fix: After making the first loop, wrap the other lace around it and tuck it through before pulling tight.

🐞

BUG 2: Steps to brush teeth

1Put toothpaste on brush
2Brush for 2 minutes
3Rinse mouth
4Put toothbrush away

Bug: Missing "wet the brush" before toothpaste, and missing "spit" after brushing!

Fix: 1. Wet brush. 2. Add toothpaste. 3. Brush. 4. Spit. 5. Rinse. 6. Put away.

🐞

BUG 3: Steps to get dressed

1Put on shirt
2Put on socks
3Put on pants
4Put on shoes

Bug: Socks go on AFTER pants! Otherwise you can't pull your pants over your socks easily.

Fix: 1. Shirt. 2. Pants. 3. Socks. 4. Shoes.
