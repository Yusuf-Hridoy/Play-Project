You Are The Computer

A computer does EXACTLY what you tell it. Nothing more. Nothing less.

Follow these steps in order. Do not skip. Do not guess.
1 Draw a dot in the center of the box below.
2 Draw a line 3 cm up from the dot.
3 Draw a line 3 cm right from the dot.
4 Draw a line 3 cm down from the dot.
5 Draw a line 3 cm left from the dot.
6 Connect the ends of the lines.
Drawing Box: 4 in × 4 in
Click and drag to draw. Each grid square = 1 cm.

🎯 What did you make?

🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Now try: Give these steps to a friend. Did they make the same thing?

🔍 If NO, what went wrong?

💡 Hint

Computers need every detail.
"Draw a square" is not enough.
"Draw a line 3 cm up, then 3 cm right" is better.

Order Matters

Computers follow steps in EXACT ORDER. Change the order = change the result.

✓

Recipe A

  1. Put bread on plate
  2. Spread peanut butter
  3. Put jelly on top
  4. Put second bread on top
🥪 Normal sandwich
✗

Recipe B (same steps, wrong order)

  1. Put jelly on plate
  2. Put bread on top
  3. Spread peanut butter
  4. Put second bread on top
💥 Mess!

✈️ Your Turn

These steps make a paper airplane. They are SCRAMBLED. Click the steps in the correct order (1 → 4).

✏️ Draw It: Try making the airplane with your numbered steps. Did it fly?

🔍 If NO, which step was wrong?

💡 Remember

Computers never "figure out" what you meant.
They execute commands exactly as written, in exact sequence.

True or False

Computers make decisions by asking YES/NO questions. We call the answer TRUE or FALSE.

🔥 Warm-Up: Circle TRUE or FALSE for each

1 A triangle has 3 sides
2 Ice cream is hot
3 10 is bigger than 7
4 Dogs can fly
5 Your name has letters in it

🎭 Tricky Ones: These depend on RIGHT NOW

6 I am wearing socks
7 It is nighttime
8 I have eaten breakfast today

🤔 Why are 6-8 different?

Answer: The answer CHANGES based on when you ask! Computers need to check every time.

If This, Then That

Computers make decisions using IF and ELSE.

⚡ Try It Live

IF it is raining
THEN take an umbrella
ELSE
wear sunglasses
Is it raining?
wear sunglasses
Real code looks like this:
IF it is raining
   THEN take an umbrella
ELSE
   wear sunglasses
